Avocado Toast

fast and delicious toast for the whole family

Ingreaients

  • 100 ml milk
  • 50g butter
  • 150g pumpkin
  • 1tbs sugar
  • 2tsp cinnamon
  • a pinch of salt
  • 30 leaves of thyme
  • 100ml oat milk
  • 2tbs ground cinnamon
  • a pinch of salt

Discription

Avocado toast is a simple, open-faced sandwich made by mashing ripe avocado onto toasted bread and typically seasoning it with salt, pepper, and a squeeze of lemon or lime juice. It's a quick, customizable dish that can be enhanced with various toppings like olive oil, chili flakes, fresh herbs, a fried egg, or cheese, making it a versatile option for breakfast, lunch, or a snack
